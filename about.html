About CrowdCue

We turn the crowd into the highlight team.

CrowdCue is building a platform that captures fan perspective at scale. Our mission is to help events live forever — one moment at a time.

Why we exist

Live events are electric, but most of the magic is gone by the time you get back to your seat. We believe those memories should be captured, shared, and celebrated in the moment.

What we are building

A live moment engine that combines fan submissions, AI curation, and human storytelling to publish event recaps in minutes.

Our approach

FPO overview of how the system will work at launch.

Audience-first capture

Moment-based organization

Clips are grouped into moments as the event unfolds.

Instant distribution

Export highlight reels for social, screens, and recaps.
