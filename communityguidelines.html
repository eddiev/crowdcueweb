Community Guidelines

v1.0 · Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Shared Moments Start with Shared Respect

Stelli exists to help people experience live moments together.

Every Host and every Guest helps shape that experience.

These Community Guidelines explain the behavior we expect from everyone using Stelli and help create an environment where people feel welcome, respected, and safe.

By using Stelli, you agree to follow these Guidelines in addition to our Terms of Service.

Respect the Moment

Every Moment is created with a purpose.

Whether you’re hosting a family gathering, celebrating a milestone, or sharing a special experience, help make each Moment enjoyable for everyone involved.

Treat other participants with kindness, courtesy, and respect.

Respect Each Other

Everyone deserves to feel welcome.

Do not:

  • Harass, bully, or intimidate others.
  • Threaten violence or encourage harm.
  • Discriminate against others.
  • Impersonate another person or organization.
  • Use Stelli to stalk, target, or repeatedly harass someone.

Record Responsibly

Stelli is built around shared experiences that may include audio and photos.

Before recording or sharing another person’s voice or image, make sure you have any permission required by applicable law.

Hosts are responsible for understanding and complying with the recording, privacy, and consent laws that apply where their Moments take place.

Guests should respect the expectations established by the Host and other participants.

Share Only What You Have the Right to Share

Only upload or contribute content that belongs to you or that you have permission to use.

This includes:

  • Audio recordings
  • Photos
  • Artwork
  • Logos
  • Music
  • Any other copyrighted material

Do not upload content that infringes another person’s intellectual property or other legal rights.

Respect Privacy

Not every Moment is intended for everyone. Respect the privacy of Hosts and Guests. Do not attempt to access Moments you are not authorized to join. Do not share another person’s personal information without their permission.

Invitations

Invite people because you genuinely want them to participate.

Do not:

  • Send unwanted or excessive invitations.
  • Mislead people about a Moment.
  • Use Stelli for spam or deceptive communications.

Hosts

Hosts play an important role in creating a positive experience.

Depending on the available features, Hosts may:

  • Invite Guests.
  • Manage participation.
  • Remove Guest-contributed photos or other eligible content from their Moments.
  • Block Guests from participating in their Moments.
  • End a Moment.

Please use these moderation tools fairly and responsibly. They are intended to help maintain a welcoming environment—not to harass, retaliate against, or unfairly target participants.

Guests

As a Guest, you’re responsible for the content you contribute and the way you participate.

Please:

  • Respect the Host and other participants.
  • Contribute thoughtfully.
  • Follow the purpose of the Moment.
  • Share only content you have permission to contribute.
  • Respect requests made by the Host regarding participation.

Help Keep Stelli Safe

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these Guidelines, please use any reporting tools available within Stelli or contact us.

We review reports and may investigate potential violations.

What Happens If These Guidelines Are Violated?

Depending on the nature and severity of a violation, Stelli may remove content, restrict access to specific features, suspend or terminate accounts, or take other action reasonably necessary to protect our users and the Stelli community.

Hosts may also use the moderation tools available within their own Moments to manage participation and maintain a respectful experience.

Our Goal

Stelli was built to help people create and experience meaningful moments together.

These Guidelines are intended to support that goal by encouraging respect, responsibility, and consideration for everyone who participates.
